Embark on a unique adventure in Odyssey: Realm of Echoes, a 3D platformer and open-world game created by a solo developer. The game features two distinct experiences: a solo adventure and a multiplayer quest, each with its own unique mechanics and storyline.

Embark on a unique adventure in Odyssey: Realm of Echoes, a 3D platformer and open-world game created by a solo developer. The game features two distinct experiences: a solo adventure and a multiplayer quest, each with its own unique mechanics and storyline.

Odyssey is developed on the Unreal Engine 4 game engine (Version 20) and will require at minimum the following specifications:

AMD Ryzen 5 1600

GPU

RAM

16Go DDR4

Singleplayer Mode: Step into the shoes of a legendary adventurer who vanished 1,000 years ago. As the greatest hero of his time, your mission is to reclaim lost powers and uncover the mystery behind his disappearance.

Multiplayer Mode: Join other players in an expansive, open world. Explore a diverse continent made up of three unique regions, with a quest to uncover the fate of a mysterious fourth region that has vanished. Collaborate with friends or face challenges alone as you delve into the unknown.

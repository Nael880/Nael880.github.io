ODYSSEY

Type

Videogame

Language

UE4 Blueprint

Release

Expected 2024

"Odyssey" is a 3D platformer featuring a solo campaign that follows the journey of a character who awakens in a new world, devoid of their powers, a millennium later. Players navigate through diverse environments, overcoming obstacles and enemies while striving to reclaim their lost abilities. The game also offers a multiplayer mode, transforming into a vast multiplayer RPG experience. Players can train their characters, explore dungeons, engage in monster battles, participate in arena combat against other players, purchase weapons and gear from shops, and explore entirely unique worlds.

In the solo campaign of "Lost Powers: Reborn," players are thrust into the shoes of a protagonist who awakens to a world vastly different from the one they once knew. Bereft of their once formidable powers, they embark on a journey of self-discovery and redemption.

As players progress through the campaign, they unravel the mysteries of the world around them, encountering a diverse cast of characters who offer aid, guidance, or opposition along the way. Each interaction adds depth to the narrative, immersing players in a richly realized world teeming with history and lore.
